Our Story

Where Every Harvest
Tells a Story

Nestled on Elwood Road in the heart of East Northport, DeLea Farmstand has been a beloved neighbor and trusted source of fresh, seasonal produce for generations. We are a family business — not a chain, not a franchise — just real people who care deeply about the food they sell and the community they serve.

Every week, our stand reflects what Long Island's land offers at its best. From sun-warmed tomatoes to fragrant herbs and crisp sweet corn, our selection changes with the seasons because real food always does.
